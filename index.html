Intro

The zee community

The Zee community is a platform for artists, sportsmen and art lovers with the sole aim of creating a family run by art.

What is zee Cuture?

The Zee Culture is an interactive platform for artists and art lovers. It is a subsidiary of the larger Zee community. Its main focus is music and poetry. It is an inter-institute project that aims at the interaction of student-artists and the society through art. Our main aim is to enhance networking among the youth through the love of what we do.

What is zee Indesign?

Zee in-design is an interactive platform for designers and lovers of design works; a subsidiary of the larger Zee community. We house an inter-institute project that aims at the interaction of student-designers and the society through design. Our main aim is to mobilise the youth and let every individual develop skill, learn, share, grow and network.

What is Revolution Zee?

The Zee Revolution is an interactive platform for sports men, women and its lovers. It is a subsidiary of the larger Zee community. Its main focus is biking and skating. It is an inter-institute project that aims at the interaction of students and the society through sports. Our main aim is to enhance networking among the youth through the love of what we do.
